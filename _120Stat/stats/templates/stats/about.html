What is 120stat?

120stat is a website designed to help fantasy football players make informed decisions before drafting, trading, or claiming players. The application utilizes nfldb, an open source project that provides a database and Python interface to gather NFL statistics. Our project is currently a work in progress. It can be found at nfldb.

Who are the creators?

The four members of our team are George Tsitsopoulos, Shane O'Brien, Zach Maurer, and Jose Guvenilir. We are all seniors at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute working together in our Software Design and Documentation class.
